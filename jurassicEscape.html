🦕 JURASSIC ESCAPE 🦖

Survive endless waves of dinosaurs. Collect weapons, heal, and tame raptors to fight back.

🎮 Controls

  • W A S D Move
  • SPACE Attack
  • Q Cycle weapon
  • R Dodge roll (costs stamina, invincible)
  • SHIFT Tame nearby raptor (max 2 allies)
  • Y Call 2 raptors (30s cooldown, max 2)
  • All pickups are collected automatically by walking over them.

⚔️ Weapons

  • 👊 Fists — always available, short range
  • 🪓 Club — big knockback, good for space
  • 🔱 Spear — very long melee reach
  • ⚔️ Sword — high damage, fast attacks
  • 🏹 Bow & Arrow — ranged, keeps you safe
  • Melee hits knock enemies back. Cycle with Q.

🎒 Pickups

  • 🍖 Food — restores 25 HP
  • 💊 Medicine — restores 50 HP
  • 🛡️ Shield — blocks 3 hits, then breaks
  • 🪓 ⚔️ 🔱 🏹 Weapons — upgrade your arsenal
  • Weapons auto-cycle to the new one when picked up.
🦖 Dinosaurs

Early Waves

  • 🔴 Raptor — fast, aggressive. Can be tamed with SHIFT when nearby.
  • 🔵 Pterodactyl — fast flyer, dives from range.
  • 🟡 Dilophosaurus — spits venom from a distance, keeps its range.
  • 🟠 Pachycephalosaurus — stops, winds up (flashing ring), then charges at speed 9. Rams stun you for 0.5s and knock you back. 5s cooldown between charges.

Later Waves

  • 🟢 Triceratops — medium speed, strong melee hit.
  • 🟦 Stegosaurus — slow tank with back plates, heavy damage.
  • 🟣 Spinosaurus — large chomper with sail, very high damage.
  • 🟤 Ankylosaurus — 400 HP armored fortress, club tail. Very slow but nearly unstoppable.
  • 🟣 T-Rex — massive HP, devastating hits. Avoid until well-armed.
